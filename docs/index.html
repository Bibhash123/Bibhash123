Hello !! I am Bibhash, a Final Year undergraduate @ NIT Rourkela, India, majoring in Electronics and Instrumentation Engineering with a Minor in Computer Science and Engineering. My major interest lies in the field of Machine Learning. I have experience as a Student Researcher in Deep Learning, having worked at research groups based in University College Dublin, Ireland and NIT Rourkela, India

Having first encountered the field of Machine Learning and Data Science during my freshmen year, my interest in these field has grown manyfolds. After reading multiple research works, completing multiple MOOC's, books and related course subjects, I can say that my knowledge in the field has grown significantly. I have taken part in numerous competitions and hackathons and have done multiple projects in ML. I have research experience in the area of Image Forensics, and Deep Learning in Satellite based remote sensing. I am also a notebooks expert on Kaggle.

My primary interest lies in implementing solutions for challenging real world problems. I take interest in studying research papers related to new developments in the fields of Computer Vision, NLP and ML & DL in general.
